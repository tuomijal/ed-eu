Equal-Weight Mean (EWM)

\[
\bar{x}_i = \frac{1}{n_i} \sum_{j=1}^{n_i} x_{ij}
\]

\[
\bar{x}_{\text{equal}} = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} \bar{x}_i

Population-Standardized Mean (PSM)

% Global population-weighted mean
\[
\bar{Y}_{\text{global}} = \sum_{j=1}^{J} w_j \, \bar{Y}_j
\]

% Country-level mean
\[
\bar{Y}_j = \frac{1}{n_j} \sum_{i=1}^{n_j} Y_{ij}
\]

% Population weight
\[
w_j = \frac{P_j}{\sum_{k=1}^{J} P_k}
